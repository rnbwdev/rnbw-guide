thank you for choosing rnbw as your design and development tool. rnbw designed to be practical and intuitive and to provide you with hours of creative flow. to get the most out of rnbw, read this operator's manual carefully. for additional information and professional tips, visit the rnbw community website.

before we begin, it's important to note that this guide serves a dual purpose - not only is it a resource for you to learn rnbw. it is also the tool for us to develop and design rnbw. we use rnbw to design this guide and improve rnbw continuously. our goal is to explain it in a way that is easy for our users to understand and then put it into practice.
